Documentation

Volume

id: Each volume is represented as a JSON object, identified by a unique id[string], pageCount[integer], and pages [array of page objects].

Page

Pages are contained within volumes, they have a page number, and information on their header, body, and footer.

Page-level information:

pageNumber: The page number. Numbers are always sequential from the start, so in theory, updates to the OCR that add or remove pages, the page number will change. With that caveat, the practical likelihood of changes is low, so this can cautiously be used as an id.

lineCount: Number of non-empty lines in the page.

emptyLineCount: Number of empty lines in the page.

sentenceCount: Number of sentences identified in the text. [link-to-faq Details on parsing].

Header, Body, and Footer information

The fields for headerStats, bodyStats, and footerStats are the same, but apply to different parts of the page.

tokenCount: The number of unique tokens in the section.

lineCount: The number of lines containing text in the section. This represents the layout of the page, for sentence counts, see the sentenceCount field.

emptyLineCount: The number of lines without text.

sentenceCount: Sentences found in the text, parsed by …...

tokens: A list of all tokens, counted by their part of speech usage. Details on POS parsing and types of tags used.

beginLineChars: Counts of all non-zero occurring characters at the start of lines. Why?

endLineChars: Counts of all non-zero occurring characters at the end of lines. Why?
